About Medovika Bakers

Inspired by Russian traditions and driven by a love for baking, Medovika Bakers brings a unique fusion menu to the heart of Varanasi. From layered cakes to iced frappes, every item is made fresh and with love.

All items are freshly made and 100% vegetarian!
